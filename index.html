💡 Enhancement Ideas
  • Sound Effects: Buzz-in chime, countdown beep, correct/wrong answer sounds for drama and engagement.
  • Confetti Animation: Celebrate correct answers with a confetti burst to reward participants visually.
  • Team Mode: Split participants into two teams with shared scores and alternating turn logic.
  • Power-Ups: "Double Points" or "50/50 Hint" tokens participants can earn and spend strategically.
  • Chat & Reactions: A sidebar live chat with emoji reactions (🙌 🔥 🤔) to build energy during play.
  • Multi-Round Game: Define rounds per Bible Testament (OT / NT), with a wrap-up screen showing full stats.
  • Streak Bonuses: Award bonus points for consecutive correct answers to reward consistent performers.
  • Mobile PWA: Package as a Progressive Web App so participants can install it on their phones.
  • Backend Sync: Use Firebase Realtime Database or Supabase for true multi-device real-time sync (currently simulated locally).
